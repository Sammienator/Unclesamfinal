We are a family friendly, food and beverage establishment establishmet in the year 2010, registered and licensed under the Kajiado county business act. We seek to provide not only the best service in food and drink, but also to provide an alternative to the already saturated food market in Kenya. We are profecient in general Kenyan cuisine, both modern and traditional,with our dedicated chefs ready to take up whatever challenge that comes their way. our staff in total is 294, spread out acrross all three restautants,making this not just a business, but an extended family!


Welcome to Uncle sam restautants, your number one source for all things food and fingerliking treats]. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of food service, with a focus on three characteristics, ie: dependability, customer service and uniqueness.Founded in 2019 , UncleSam has come a long way from its beginnings in a home office in Kitengela. When uncle sam first started out, his passion for food and exemplary cuisine drove him to start this restaurant, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming venture.
